\begin{exercise}
 We study two sequences that are closely related. 
 
Suppose that $\{a_n \}_{n=1}$ where $a_n = \ln\left(n^{1/n}\right)$.  Then:
\[
\lim_{n \to \infty} a_n = \answer{0}
\]
