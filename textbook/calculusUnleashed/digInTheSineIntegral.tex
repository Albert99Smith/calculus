Here we will use facts about calculus to investigate a ``slippary''
function, one that is hard to get our hands on.

\begin{definition}
  The \dfn{sine integral} is most commonly defined as
  \[
  \mathrm{Si}(x) = \int_0^x \frac{\sin(t)}{t}\d t.
  \]
\end{definition}
