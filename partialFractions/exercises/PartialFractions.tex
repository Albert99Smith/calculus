\begin{exercise}


We want to decompose the following fraction: 
$\frac{1}{(x+5)(x+12)^2} $

$
\frac{1}{(x+5)(x+12)^2} =
 \answer{\frac{-1}{49(x+12)}} +
\answer{\frac{-1}{7(x+12)^2}} + 
\answer{\frac{1}{49(x+5)}}
$
\end{exercise}
